Privacy Policy for Converter

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection
   Converter does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. All currency conversions are performed using the Frankfurter API, and no user-specific data is sent to the API provider.

2. Local Storage
   The app may use local storage on your device (AsyncStorage) solely to remember your theme preference (Light/Dark mode). This data remains on your device and is not accessible by the developer.

3. Third-Party Services
   The app uses the Frankfurter API to fetch exchange rates. No personal identifiers are shared with this service.

4. Changes to This Policy
   We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
